MY FIRST DAY OF COLLEGE

lovely day, JULY 7, 2022

Excitement pulsated as I stepped onto the college campus, a tapestry of unfamiliar faces and bustling activity. Nervous anticipationmingled with the thrill of newfound independence, echoing through the lecture halls and corridors. Professors' introductions and syllabus details set the academic tone,while chance encounters with future friends marked the beginning of lasting connections. The aroma of cafeteria coffee and the echo of laughter encapsulated the memorable mosaic of my inaugural college experience, a vivid snapshot in the album of my educational journey.
